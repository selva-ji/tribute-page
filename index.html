K.Kamaraj

The King Maker , former Chief Minister of Tamilnadu

Here's a time line of Mr.K.Kamaraj's life:

  • 1903-Born in virudhunagar,Tamilnadu,India.
  • 1914-Father died,dropped out of school to support his family.
  • 1920-Joined congress became full-time political worker.
  • 1930-Went to jail for participating in the 'salt satyagragha'.
  • 1937-Entered Assembly winning the sattur seat in the 1937 Election.
  • 1954-Became the chief minister of Madras province,immediately removed hereditary education policy.
  • 1956-Introduced midday meal scheme in all schools in the state.
  • 1963-Resigned from the post of Chief Minister.Became the President of Indian National Congress.
  • 1964-After the death of Nehru he made Lal Bahadur Shastri as the Prime Minister of India.
  • 1966-After the death of Lal Bahadur Shastri he made Indira Gandhi as as the Prime Minister of India.For this role,he was widely acclaimed as the 'Kingmaker' during 1960s
  • 1975-He died at his home on Gandhi jayanthi in his sleep due to heart attack.he did not marry,did not won any property and never tempted by power,when he died heft behind 130 rupees,2 pair of sandals, 4 shirts,4 dhotis, and few books.
  • 1976-Awarded 'Bharat Ratna posthumously.
